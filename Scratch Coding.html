In this page I'll show you how to code character movement in scratch.

Start by opening the scratch.mit.edu website, then press create.

Press on the events tab

Drag the "when gf clicked" block onto the page

On the "control" tab, drag the "forever" loop onto the green flag block, then, inside the forever loop, add two "if then" loops, make sure they're on top of each other as shown, and not inside each other.

Open the "sensing" tab and drag 2 "" blocks into the diamond shaped area of the block.

Press on the (space) dropdown of the " blocks and change the top one to (right arrow) and the bottom one to (left arrow)

In the motion tab, drag two "move 10 steps blocks", change the value of the bottom one to -10

On the right of your screen you will see a box. This is your stage, above your stage there will be a green flag and red stop sign. Press on the green flag. You will now be able to use your left and right arrows to move the cat! (jC+tr)10)4" git
